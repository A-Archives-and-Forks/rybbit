---
title: Make your analytics public
description: Learn how to make your analytics dashboard public and share it with others
---

import Image from "next/image";


You can make your analytics public by going to the settings page and enabling the "Public Analytics" option. You can then share the page link with anyone you want. 

- External viewers will not be able to edit any of your settings or add/remove/edit any existing reports, funnels, or goals. 
- Your other websites will not be affected.
