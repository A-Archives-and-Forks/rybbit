# Webflow

Rybbit enables you to collect analytics, capture custom events, record sessions, and deploy feature flags on your Webflow site.

## Installation

1. **Get your JavaScript snippet**
   Navigate to your Rybbit project settings and copy the provided web snippet. It should resemble the following:

   ```html
   <script src="YOUR_INSTANCE_ADDRESS" async data-site-id="YOUR_SITE_ID"></script>
   ```

2. **Add the snippet to Webflow**

  - In your Webflow dashboard, open your project and navigate to **Project Settings**.
  - Go to the **Custom Code** tab.
  - Paste the Rybbit snippet into the **Head Code** section.
  - Click **Save Changes**.
  - Publish your site to apply the changes.
